THE
EXHIBITION

An exhibition by DOC Studio

We are both obsolete reflects on the shared obsolescence of technology and the human condition in the digital age. Through newly developed works, it invites critical engagement with the psychological, social, and environmental costs of technological progress.

The works span modified interfaces, sculptural assemblage, and degraded digital aesthetics. Physical and digital materials become entangled: printed circuit boards sit alongside brushstrokes, while coded behaviours interrupt sculptural forms—blurring the boundary between technological debris and artistic gesture.

Please explore the systems that make both machines and people disposable: planned obsolescence, algorithmic burnout, fractured attention, and the quiet violence of constant upgrades. Visitors are invited to reflect on a culture where relevance is engineered to expire by design, not necessity.
